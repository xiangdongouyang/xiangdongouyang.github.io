How We Make 1–50pcs EV Aluminum Housings with CNC Prototype

We specialize in low-volume prototype from 1 to 50 pieces for EV aluminum housing parts, mainly serving automotive customers from USA and Germany. Aluminum 6061 and AlSi12Fe are our most commonly used raw materials for cooling housing prototype.

Before machining, our engineering team completes full DFM analysis to optimize structure and reduce unnecessary manufacturing cost for small batch orders. The whole procedure includes raw material cutting, CNC milling, tapping, surface anodizing and final dimensional inspection.
