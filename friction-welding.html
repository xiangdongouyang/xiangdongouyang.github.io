Friction Welding Services

We provide high-strength friction welding services for aluminum and dissimilar metal assemblies. This solid-state welding process produces no porosity, no defects, and excellent airtight performance, making it ideal for pressure vessels, heat exchangers, and structural components.

Capabilities

  • Linear & rotary friction welding
  • Aluminum, copper, steel dissimilar material welding
  • High airtight performance (zero leakage)
  • High strength weld joint, no porosity
  • Customized welding solutions for complex assemblies

Industries We Serve

  • Hydraulic components
  • Heat exchangers
  • Aerospace structural parts
  • Medical equipment
