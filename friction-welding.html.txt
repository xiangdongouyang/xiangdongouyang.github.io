<div class="section">
  <h2>Friction Welding</h2>
  <p>Friction welding is a solid-state welding process that produces high-quality, high-strength joints without melting the material. It is ideal for joining dissimilar metals and creating airtight, leak-proof assemblies for pressure and fluid components.</p>
  
  <h3>Advantages</h3>
  <div class="advantage">
    <span>Airtight joints</span>
    <span>No porosity</span>
    <span>High strength</span>
    <span>Clean process (no filler material)</span>
    <span>Stable mass production</span>
    <span>Dissimilar metal joining</span>
  </div>

  <h3>Capabilities</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Rotary friction welding</li>
    <li>Linear friction welding</li>
    <li>Aluminum to aluminum welding</li>
    <li>Aluminum to steel welding</li>
    <li>Tube and bar welding</li>
  </ul>
