About Us

X.d Prototype is a professional manufacturer focusing on low-volume 1~50pcs prototype for automotive and EV components. We provide one-stop solution including CNC machining, friction welding, sheet metal, plastic molding, airtight testing and independent jig & fixture design.

We accept external custom design orders with full service: 3D/2D drawing, DFM modification and small-batch sample production. We mainly serve clients from USA, Germany and Europe markets with strict quality control and fast lead time.
